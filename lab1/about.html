About Book Explorer

Book Explorer is your gateway to discovering literature that inspires and entertains. Our goal is to help readers easily browse, favorite, and explore new books.

Our Mission

Book Explorer is dedicated to making book discovery simple, enjoyable, and accessible to everyone. We believe that finding your next great read should be an adventure, not a chore. By leveraging the power of the Open Library API, we bring you access to millions of books with powerful search and personalization features.

Smart Search

Find books by title, author, or subject with our powerful search powered by Open Library

Personal Favorites

Save your favorite books and build your personal reading list that persists across sessions

Vast Collection

Access millions of books from classics to contemporary bestsellers in every genre

Community Driven

Built with modern web technologies and powered by the open-source Open Library project

Technology Stack

Book Explorer is built with modern web technologies including Next.js, React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. We use the Open Library API to provide access to comprehensive book data.

This project demonstrates responsive design, API integration, local storage persistence, and modern JavaScript patterns.
